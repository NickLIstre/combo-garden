About Us

Hi! My name is Nyko (or Nick if you know me irl)

I am the founder of Black Baccara Productions and also the only member!


I am a solo developer who has been working on this project for a while now. My goal is to create a way for lovers of fighting games to easily find combos for their favorite characters.


I started this project because I noticed that each fighting game has its own unique way and place to find combos, and it can be difficult to find a consistent format across different games. I wanted to create a platform that would make it easy for players to find and learn combos, regardless of the game they are playing.


This is my first time creating a website from scratch so things are not as clean as I would like them to be. I plan on revamping the site in the future to allow for users to upload their own combos and create accounts.


I love making things interactive and accessible so look forward to improvements in the future such as combos of the month, profile customization, and more!


I am always open to feedback and suggestions, so please feel free to reach out to me through the Contact page if you have any ideas or questions!


Thank you for visiting Combo Garden! I hope you find it useful and enjoyable.
