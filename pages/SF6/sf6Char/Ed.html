5MP Meterless Starter

Notation:

5MP~HP > 214HP

5MP~HP > KK~dl.6P, 236LK

5MP~HP > KK~dl.6P, 623HP

Basic meterless BNB. Works anywhere. Use 236LK for corner carry but less damage. Use 623HP for more damage but does not provide safejump.

Notation:

j.HP, 5HK, 5MP~HP > 214HP (>236236P)

j.HP, 5MP, 2LK > 623MP

Basic jump-in combo. 5MP easy to hit confirm with 2LK.

Most Damage Meterless Jump-In

Most damaging meterless jump in.
